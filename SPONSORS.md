# Sponsor NativeBlade

NativeBlade is free and 100% open source, and it stays that way. The code is the
code: there is no paid tier hidden behind a license, no closed core, no feature
held back for a "pro" edition. Everything you can build today, you can build
forever, for free.

What sponsorship buys is not access to code. It buys the time that keeps the
project healthy: bug fixes, new native APIs, documentation, and answering the
hard questions when you ship to production. If NativeBlade saves you weeks of
work, sponsoring is how you keep that going for everyone.

There are two separate things on this page. **Sponsorship** funds the project and
gets you recognition. **Business Support** is a paid product with a written
response commitment for teams running NativeBlade in production. They are not the
same thing, and one is not a substitute for the other. If you need a guaranteed
response time, see [Business Support](SUPPORT.md).

---

## For individual developers

For people who use NativeBlade and want to keep it alive. No deliverables, no
service commitment, just support for the project and a bit of recognition.

### Supporter — $5 / month

- Sponsor badge in the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/Vzpach5J2h)
- Your name listed on the sponsors page
- The good feeling of funding open source

### Pro — $15 / month

Everything in Supporter, plus:

- A dedicated channel in Discord where sponsor questions are answered first.
  This is best effort, not a guaranteed response time. For an actual commitment,
  see Business Support.
- Early access to beta releases and release candidates
- A vote in occasional roadmap polls

---

## For companies

For teams and businesses that depend on NativeBlade. These tiers are about
recognition and a seat at the table. For a response commitment, pair them with
[Business Support](SUPPORT.md).

### Sponsor — $250 / month

- Your logo on the project README and website, in the sponsors section
- Sponsor badge in Discord for your team members
- Listed as a company sponsor

### Partner — $1,000 / month

Everything in Sponsor, plus:

- Prominent logo placement on the website home page and documentation header
- A monthly roadmap update and a direct line to discuss priorities. This is
  influence on direction, not control over it.
- Recognition as a founding partner of the project

Partner sponsors are the companies the project grows with. As NativeBlade Cloud
takes shape, partners are the first conversation we have about it.

## Sponsored features

Need a specific native API, plugin, or integration that does not exist yet? That
is custom work, not a subscription. The shape is simple:

1. You describe what you need.
2. We agree on scope and a fixed price.
3. It gets built, merged into the open source project, and stays free for
   everyone afterward.

This keeps NativeBlade fully open while letting you fund the exact thing you
need. Reach out to scope it.
