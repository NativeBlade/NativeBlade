# Business Support

NativeBlade is free and open source, and the community channels are open to
everyone. Business Support is a paid product for teams that run NativeBlade in
production and need a guaranteed response, a private channel, and direct access
to the people who build the framework.

This page describes what Business Support is, what it covers, and what it does
not. It is written plainly on purpose. A support agreement only works when both
sides know exactly what was promised.

---

## What it is

A paid subscription that gives your team:

- A **private support channel** (Discord, Slack, or email, your choice)
- A **written response time** during business hours
- Help from the maintainers of NativeBlade, not a queue of volunteers
- A **license key** that validates your team's access, issued through Polar

Business Support is separate from sponsorship. Sponsoring the project funds its
development and gets recognition. Business Support is a commercial agreement with
a service level. You can do either, both, or neither.

---

## Plans

### Business — $500 / month

- Private support channel for up to 5 named team members
- Response within **2 business days**
- Help with integration, configuration, build issues, and debugging your use of
  NativeBlade
- Priority triage on bugs that block your team

### Partner Support — $2,000 / month

Everything in Business, plus:

- Response within **1 business day**
- Up to 15 named team members
- One **office-hours call per month** (60 minutes) for architecture and planning
- Direct input into the roadmap and early visibility into upcoming changes
- A named point of contact

Business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 18:00 Central European Time
(CET/CEST, Spain), excluding public holidays. Response time means time to a
first substantive reply, not time to a full resolution.

---

## What is covered

- Questions about using NativeBlade: configuration, components, directives,
  navigation, native APIs, builds, and publishing
- Debugging problems in your integration with the framework
- Guidance on architecture and best practices for your app
- Investigation and prioritization of bugs in NativeBlade itself
- Help reading and applying the documentation

## What is not covered

Business Support is support for the framework. It is not a development team for
your application. The following are out of scope:

- Writing your application's features or business logic
- Building custom plugins or native APIs on request (that is a
  [sponsored feature](SPONSORS.md#sponsored-features), scoped and priced
  separately)
- Debugging code unrelated to NativeBlade
- Guaranteed delivery dates for fixes or features
- Support for forks or modified builds of NativeBlade
- 24/7 or on-call coverage

If you need something outside this scope, that is fine. We will tell you so and,
when it makes sense, quote it as separate work.

---

## How it works

1. Choose a plan and subscribe on
   [polar.sh/nativeblade](https://polar.sh/nativeblade).
2. You receive a license key and an invitation to your private channel.
3. Send us the names and contact handles of your team members.
4. You start getting answers within the response time of your plan.

Billing is monthly through Polar, which acts as merchant of record and handles
invoicing and VAT. You can cancel at any time; access continues until the end of
the paid period.
